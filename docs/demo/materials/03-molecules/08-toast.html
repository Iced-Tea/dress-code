--- notes: | Toasts are used to give feedback about user action, but they are simpler than messages in that they have less information and no interaction. Toasts have no default position, you must choose between top or bottom using: - `.dc-toast-container--top` plus `.dc-toast--top` to make it appear from the top - `.dc-toast-container--bottom` plus `.dc-toast--bottom` to make it appear from the bottom Note: the animation duration is not defined by the dress code and must be passed by you. ---
