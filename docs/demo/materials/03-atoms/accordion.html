--- name: Accordion title: accordion notes: | Use accordions to separate content into logical sections. `dc-accordion-input` `dc-accordion-header` and `dc-accordion__item` should be child nodes of `dc-accordion__item` and in same heirarchy to their parent node. Atleast one visible `label` should change `checked` state of each hidden `dc-accordion-input`. ---
